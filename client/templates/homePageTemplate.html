Kusuma Satya Convention

In a sprawing 2.5 acres with furnished AC rooms , specious car parking and it also has extended lawn area for live buffet. Ideal for exhibitions trade fairs , conferences , meetings and for marraige events.

It is state of the art purpose built convention centre with pre-function hall, hall-1 & hall-2. Pre-function hall with capacity of 150 - 200 members for minor events. Both the halls can be clubed for more space required events.

Hall-1 is 2000 - 2500 members capacity for various events( marriages, birthday celebrations ) with movable partition facility, it has dinning hall with a seating capacity of 300 members at a time and also 6 fully furnished AC rooms ,extended lawn area with water fountain for live buffet.

Hall-2 is 800 - 1000 members capacity for various events( marriages, birthday celebrations ) with movable partition facility, it has dinning hall with a seating capacity of 100 members at a time and also 2 fully furnished AC rooms ,extended lawn area for live buffet.
